Our Story · Grilled to perfection

Two spits, one crown, and the last light on Euclid.

Shawarma Queen does one thing and wears the crown for it: beef and chicken turned slowly on the spit, shaved thin off the edges, and rolled to order. Twelve inches or a full twenty-four — you decide how hungry you are.

We open when the day winds down and stay lit long after the rest of the block goes dark — every night until 3:30 am.
