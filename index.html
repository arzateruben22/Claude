Nuestra Historia · Our Story

Tortillas a mano, fuego lento, y la puerta siempre abierta.

Los Güeros is a family-run taqueria on La Palma Avenue in Anaheim. We do things the slow way on purpose: tortillas pressed by hand, meats over open flame, salsas made every morning in the molcajete.

Come for a quick taco at the counter or bring the whole family. Either way, you eat well — como debe ser.
