The Coach

Started from zero.

I wasn't an athlete. After high school I had no momentum — no school, no money, no real direction. Video games were the only place I felt progress, because leveling up was the fastest way to see myself getting somewhere. Then one day I walked into a gym, and everything clicked. Real progress. Progress you carry with you.

I understood something from day one: the more you put in, the more you get out. Two sessions a week is 8 in a month. Six a week is 24 — that's two entire weeks of work someone else simply doesn't have, in the same month, on the same calendar.

That's why I coach the way I do. I know what it's like to start from zero — no background, no roadmap, nothing but the decision to show up. If I can build from there, so can you. My job is to make sure every session you put in actually pays out.
