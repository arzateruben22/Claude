    FAQ

    Before you ask.

    Something else on your mind? Text (714) 353-3126 or email arzateruben22@gmail.com — you'll hear back from me, not an autoresponder.

    I've never really trained before. Can I start?

    That's the point. I started from zero — no athletic background, no plan handed to me. Foundation exists exactly for this: structure and a real starting point, with me beside you for every rep.

    Where do we train?

    A private gym in Anaheim — one-on-one, no crowd, no waiting on equipment. You get the address when you book your first session.

    Do I have to pay it all upfront?

    No. Every plan can be split: about half to get started, then two weekly payments across your first two weeks of training. Same total, nothing extra — Foundation, for example, is $199 to start, then two payments of $100. Flip the "Split it" switch above the plans to see your numbers.

    Am I locked into a contract?

    No. Every plan is month-to-month and you can cancel anytime. The results are what keep people here, not the paperwork.

    How do I get started?

    Text or call (714) 353-3126. We'll talk about your goal, pick the right tier, and book your first session — that's where we find your starting numbers and build the plan.
