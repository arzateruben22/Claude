Privacy Policy

Lumevina Aesthetics Spa · Woodland Hills, California · Last updated July 2026

Lumevina Aesthetics Spa ("Lumevina," "we") provides skincare and waxing services, and this website and mobile app let you book appointments, purchase gift certificates, and take part in our Glow Rewards program. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

  • Booking details — your name, email address, the services you select, and your appointment date and time.
  • Payment information — payments are processed by Stripe. Your card number goes directly to Stripe and is never stored on our servers; we keep only a record of the amount paid and Stripe's payment reference.
  • Glow Rewards data — your points balance and activity, your referral code, and, if you choose to share it, your birthday month.
  • Intake information — the forms you complete before treatments, used solely to provide your service safely.

How we use it

  • To schedule and confirm your appointments and process deposits.
  • To run Glow Rewards — points, streaks, birthday bonuses, and referrals.
  • To send booking confirmations and, if you opt in, appointment reminders and flash-opening notifications. You can turn notifications off any time in your device settings.
  • To respond when you contact us.

What we don't do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not share it with third parties except the service providers that make the product work (payment processing, email delivery, hosting), each bound to use it only for that purpose.
  • We do not use third-party advertising or cross-app tracking.

Your choices

You can book as a guest without creating an account. You may ask us to correct or delete your information at any time — email us and we'll take care of it. Deleting your account removes your rewards balance and history.

Retention & security

We keep booking records only as long as needed for scheduling, accounting, and the policies described at booking. Data is transmitted over encrypted connections and stored with access limited to Lumevina.

Children

Our services and app are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their information.
