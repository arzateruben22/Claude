Questions, answered

The little things worth knowing before your first visit.

Do I need to pay when I book?

A 50% deposit holds your time and goes straight toward your treatment. It keeps the calendar fair for everyone.

What if I need to reschedule?

Life happens — reschedule up to 24 hours before and your deposit moves with you, no fuss.

How do I know which treatment to pick?

You don’t have to. Book the Custom Facial and we’ll tailor everything in person after a proper consult.

Are the actives safe for sensitive skin?

Every plan is personalized and patch-tested where needed. We start gentle and build only as your skin allows.

Do gift certificates expire?

Never. Whether it’s a specific treatment or a value toward any service, it’s yours until you use it.
