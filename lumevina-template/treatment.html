Before you book

The questions we hear most about the Custom Facial.

How often should I come in?

Every four weeks keeps results compounding as your skin cycle turns over. We’ll tune the cadence to your skin and goals.

Does it hurt?

No. Extractions are done gently and only as needed — most clients find the whole hour genuinely relaxing.

Is it safe for sensitive skin?

Yes. Every step is chosen for your skin that day and patch-tested where needed. We start gentle and build only as your skin allows.

Can I wear makeup afterward?

Best to let your skin breathe tonight. If you must, keep it minimal — and always finish with SPF the next morning.

What’s the deposit when I book?

A 50% deposit holds your time and goes straight toward the treatment. Reschedule up to 24 hours before and it moves with you.
